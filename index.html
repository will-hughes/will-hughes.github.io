MSc programme calculators

These resources apply specifically and solely to MSc programmes in the School of Construction Management and Engineering.

SCME MSc award calculator

Enter all marks against each credit weighting, perhaps guessing those not yet completed, to get an accurate calculation of what degree would be awarded for those specific marks. This is based on programme specifications dates 2014-15 and later.

SCME MSc assessment date calculator

Enter a module week number to see the date the module commences and the dates for submission of assignments and feedback of marks, based on default values. Please note that, for operational reasons, these may not be the actual dates used in all modules. Assignment briefs should always be checked for submission dates.
